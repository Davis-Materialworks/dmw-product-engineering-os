# Code of Conduct

DMW Product Engineering OS is a professional, public collaboration space. Participants are expected to act with respect, clarity, and good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas, designs, architecture, and evidence rather than people.
- Prefer specific, actionable feedback over vague criticism.
- Respect different experience levels and professional backgrounds.
- Keep discussions focused on improving product engineering quality.
- Disclose relevant conflicts of interest when appropriate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Misrepresenting affiliation, authority, certification, or endorsement.
- Adding third-party proprietary content without rights or permission.
- Ignoring project trademark, security, or disclosure policies.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, close discussions, restrict participation, or take other reasonable actions to protect the project and its community.

## Reporting

Report concerns through repository issues if appropriate, or through Davis Materialworks' official contact channels for private matters.
