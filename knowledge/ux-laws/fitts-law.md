# Fitts's Law

## Principle
Targets are faster to acquire when they are larger and closer.

## Cite When
Cite when touch targets, primary actions, destructive controls, or mobile affordances are too small or distant.

## XD Critique Pattern
- Identify the affected user decision or action.
- Explain the likely cognitive, motor, or trust cost.
- Recommend a design change that preserves intent while reducing friction.
