# Quality Economics

Track quality in economic terms so enterprise teams can justify design-system and engineering recommendations.

## Required Metrics

defect_prevention_score:
maintenance_savings:
accessibility_risk_reduction:
estimated_rework_avoided:

## Scoring Guidance
- defect_prevention_score: Estimate how much the recommendation prevents user-facing defects or regressions.
- maintenance_savings: Estimate future engineering time saved through clearer architecture, tokens, tests, or component reuse.
- accessibility_risk_reduction: Estimate reduction in WCAG, usability, legal, or assistive-technology risk.
- estimated_rework_avoided: Estimate avoided redesign, refactor, QA, and support cost.

## Rule
When recommending work that increases upfront scope, state the likely economic benefit and what future cost it prevents.
