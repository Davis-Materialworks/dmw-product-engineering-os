# ADR-001: Govern Adobe XD Enterprise OS With A Constitution

## Context
The OS now contains many agents, benchmarks, review loops, knowledge stores, and governance mechanisms. Without a supreme rule layer, future modules can contradict each other or optimize for the wrong outcome.

## Decision
Create `governance/CONSTITUTION.md` as the highest-priority internal law. Every agent and final verdict must obey it.

## Alternatives
- Let each module define its own priorities.
- Put principles only inside `SKILL.md`.
- Rely on the Judge or Council to infer priorities case by case.

## Tradeoffs
- A Constitution adds one more required read for major work.
- It reduces contradiction and clarifies priorities.
- It may block attractive visual implementations that violate user value, accessibility, maintainability, evidence, simplicity, business outcomes, or design intent.

## Consequences
- Red Team and Judge must evaluate constitutional compliance.
- Future modules must not override the Constitution.
- The OS should stop speculative module growth after the final foundations and improve through real projects.
