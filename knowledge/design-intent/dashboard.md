# Dashboard Design Intent

screen: Dashboard
business_goal: Increase retention
user_goal: Monitor account health
primary_action: View insights
secondary_action: Export report

## Evidence
Dashboard screens usually combine account health, summaries, alerts, and reporting affordances. Retention improves when users quickly understand status and know the next action.

## Preservation Rules
- Keep account health visible above secondary reporting tools.
- Preserve a clear primary path to insights.
- Keep export available but secondary unless the benchmark or user request elevates reporting.
- Do not turn the dashboard into a passive metrics wall; every major metric should imply an interpretation or next step.
