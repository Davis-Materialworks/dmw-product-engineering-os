# Progressive Disclosure

pattern: Progressive Disclosure
impact: High
industry: SaaS
evidence: Repeatedly reduces cognitive load in dashboards, onboarding, settings, admin, reporting, and checkout flows by showing advanced detail only when needed.
reuse_count: 24

## Why It Worked
Users can complete primary tasks without carrying every option in working memory. Expert users still retain access to advanced controls.

## When To Reuse
- Dense dashboards
- Enterprise settings
- Admin permission flows
- Multi-step onboarding
- Checkout and payment flows
- Reporting filters

## When Not To Reuse
- Safety-critical alerts that must be visible immediately
- Legal or consent information that must not be hidden
- Primary actions whose discoverability drives completion

Track completion rate, time to task, support tickets, accessibility findings, and repeated reuse across industries.
