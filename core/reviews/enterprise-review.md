# Enterprise Review System

Run this review before finalizing major XD-to-code work.

## Product

- Is the user goal clear?
- Is the primary action obvious?
- Are empty/loading/error/success states present?
- Are destructive actions protected?

## UX

- Is hierarchy strong?
- Is cognitive load appropriate?
- Are forms understandable?
- Are data-heavy views filterable, sortable, searchable, and exportable where needed?

## Architecture

- Are components reusable?
- Are tokens used correctly?
- Is state explicit?
- Are routes and data boundaries sensible?

## Accessibility

- Does it meet WCAG 2.2 AA?
- Are keyboard and screen reader paths complete?
- Is contrast acceptable?

## Industry risk

Finance, healthcare, government, education, and enterprise systems require stronger privacy, auditability, permissions, and error recovery.
