## Adobe XD Usage

Adobe XD is the primary design source currently supported by this project because it reflects the real-world workflows used by Davis Materialworks.

For many years, Adobe Creative Cloud products have been part of the daily design and production workflow used by Davis Materialworks to create websites, applications, design systems, user interfaces, and digital products.

As a result, Adobe XD was selected as the initial design source for DMW Product Engineering OS based on practical experience and existing design assets rather than any relationship with Adobe.

## Purpose of the Project

DMW Product Engineering OS was created to improve internal workflows, design review processes, product engineering practices, and design-to-production quality standards.

Over time, the framework evolved into a reusable system that Davis Materialworks believes may also benefit designers, developers, product teams, and organizations facing similar challenges.

The project is shared publicly in the spirit of knowledge sharing, continuous improvement, and professional collaboration.

## Future Design Sources

Although Adobe XD is the initial design source supported by this repository, the architecture is intended to remain design-source agnostic.

Future support may include:

- Figma
- Penpot
- Sketch
- Design Tokens
- Product Requirement Documents
- Design System Specifications
- Additional design and product engineering workflows

The long-term objective is to build a product engineering intelligence platform rather than a platform tied to any single design tool.
